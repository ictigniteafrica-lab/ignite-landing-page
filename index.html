Why this Flagship Programme?

The Ignite AfCFTA Bootcamp empowers Africa's entrepreneurs, policymakers, and business leaders to turn policy into Wealth & Growth. While the AfCFTA is the world's largest free trade area, its true potential lies beyond agreements — it lies in execution. This BootCamp equips participants with the practical knowledge, tools, and networks to transform AfCFTA frameworks into real business opportunities, export growth, and job creation across the continent. This programme turns awareness into action.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior trade experience?

No. The programme is designed for all levels — from entrepreneurs exploring export options to trade professionals seeking applied knowledge.

What is the time commitment?

Expect 4–6 hours per week: 2–3 hours for self-paced modules and a 1–2 hour live session.

Will there be mentorship?

Yes — each cohort has peer groups and assigned mentors for practical support.

Is there certification?

Graduates receive the AfCFTA Business Champion Certificate and access to the alumni network.
